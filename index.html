From The Manager

We're Passionate About Delivering Growth Services

The Lumbini Agro-Product & Research Center Private Limited, the largest private cattle farm in Nepal. The farm has currently more than 500 heads of Holstein and Jersey cows from which we daily produce more than 2000 liter milk....
The milk produced on the farm is being supplied to Retailers & DDC. However, we plan to develop our own dairy farm in near future and also aim to become a model hybrid cow breeding center in South Asia. The farm located in Tikuligadh of Rupandehi district, about 350 kilometers southwest of Kathmandu, One of the principal objectives of the farm is to develop it into a hybrid cow breeding center. With an aim to develop livestock farming into a model project in Nepal, Lumbini Agro-Product and Research Centre Pvt. Ltd has brought the milking machines worth Rs. 2 million. In addition to milking cows using machines, the task of cutting grass is also done using machines. We are planning to produce 3,000 liters of milk daily in near future from its current production of 2000 liters. Milk produced from our farm also meets the standards set by the American and European nations. Our BioGas electricity is now complete and running. Read More
